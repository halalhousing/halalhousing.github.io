About Us

If there is a will there are multiple ways. Owning a decent flat in metropolitan cities of India through Halal means is no longer a distant dream. After so many years of patient waiting, contemplation and ground work, we are finally able to arrive at launching housing projects under the banner of Halal Housing - a novel housing scheme for the truly faithful. It is a hard reality that many of our brothers and sisters are still not in a position to have a own house in spite of a decent earning for the simple reason that they don’t want to go against the tenets of Islam by taking a conventional home loan from a Bank and fall into the traps of paying interest for the rest of their lives. Halal Housing is the result of their patient waiting and putting their complete trust in Allah.

Alhamdulillah, under this scheme we have already completed one project in Bannerghatta Road, Bangalore and we are just starting our next project in the same vicinity but in a much bigger area. Our mission is to create more and more such projects, that will make housing affordable, simple and enjoyable.

Here is the gist of this novel scheme - Pay a minimum of 36% upfront of the total cost and balance in maximum 36 EMI’s (Interest FREE) which is Three Years. For example, if the total cost of a flat is 30 lakhs, then you pay 10.80 lakhs (36%) minimum upfront payment and the balance 19.20 lakhs in maximum 36 Interest FREE EMI’s of approximately Rs.53,333/- each. We are sure this sounds very attractive and irresistible, Alhamdulillah.

Pricing & Payment

The pre-launch price per SFT is just INR 3650/- (Three Thousand Six Hundred and Fifty ONLY). Kindly note that the typical price in that area (Bannerghatta Road) for similar flats is somewhere between INR 4500/- to 7500/- depending on the builder. As we are committed to make this Halal Housing project affordable to our brothers and sisters, we are offering the flats almost at the cost price without compromising on the quality of construction and materials used. All payments should be directly made in favor of Halal Venturez. The bank account details will be provided after signing the agreement.

The other charges after the project completion include (per flat) are ONLY,

  • Covered Car Parking – 1.75 Lakhs (Fixed)
  • BESCOM + BWSSB deposits and incidental expenses - 1.90 Lakhs (Fixed)
  • Any other Taxes and Registration Charges

You will also have the option of “Flexi-Pay” that is, pay a higher initial amount and lesser EMI’s or fewer amount per EMI.
