We are extremely glad to inform you that we are all set to officially launch our fourth project named "CocoVille" in Balaji Gardens, Gottigere, Bannerghatta Road, Bangalore.

Here is the gist of this novel scheme - Pay a minimum of 35% initially of the total cost and balance in upto 48 Interest Free EMI (as low as Rs.41 Sq.Ft) . For example, if the total cost of a flat is 30 lakhs, then you pay 10.50 lakhs (35%) minimum payment (1 lakh as booking amount and rest 9.50 lakhs at the time of sale agreement) and the balance 19.50 lakhs in maximum 48 Interest FREE EMI’s. We are sure this sounds very attractive and irresistible.

    5. Furniture Layout Planning & Anthropometric guidelines

    7. Utility Area for each flat (Common Water Closet)
