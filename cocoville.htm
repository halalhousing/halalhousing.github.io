We are extremely glad to inform you that we are all set to officially launch our fourth project named "CocosVille" in Balaji Gardens, Gottigere, Bannerghatta Road, Bangalore.

Here is the gist of this novel scheme - Pay a minimum of 35% initially of the total cost and balance in upto 48 EMI’s (Interest FREE) which is Four Years. For example, if the total cost of a flat is 30 lakhs, then you pay 10.50 lakhs (35%) minimum payment (1 lakh as booking amount and rest 9.50 lakhs at the time of sale agreement) and the balance 19.50 lakhs in maximum 48 Interest FREE EMI’s of approximately Rs.40600/- each. We are sure this sounds very attractive and irresistible, Alhamdulillah.

Alhamdulillah, under this scheme we have already sold three projects, one at Old Madras Road and two other projects in Bannerghatta Road, Bangalore. Floor Plans and Elevation

The proposed apartment building will have Car Parking on the Stilt and Three Floors (Ground, First and Second). Each floor will have spacious four units of 2BHK flats measuring roughly around 1100 Square Foots. Flat-A is 1064 SFT, Flat-B is 1079 SFT, Flat-C is 1079 SFT and Flat-D is 1054 SFT. Each flat will have at least four Balconies.

While making the architectural plan we have considered the following points:

  1. Three ways In/Out gates for Car parking. Minimum 10 ft driveway space and with 2 driveways accommodating 14 to 15 cars
  2. Front Elevation to be best in class
  3. Ample Lighting and ventilation for all flats, balconies and lobby
  4. Space Planning with the Openness design concept
  5. Furniture Layout Planning & Anthropometric guidelines
  6. Spacious Kitchen and Toilets
  7. Utility Area for each flat (Common Water Closet)
  8. Separate electric transformer, power generator and security room in the stilt
  9. Prayer Room on the Terrace

Pricing & Payment

The pre-launch price per SFT is just INR 2,990/- (Two Thousand Nine Hundred and Ninety ONLY). Kindly note that the typical price in that area (Off Old Madras Road) for similar flats is somewhere between INR 3800/- to 5500/- depending on the builder. As we are committed to make this Halal Housing project affordable to our brothers and sisters, we are offering the flats almost at the cost price without compromising on the quality of construction and materials used. All payments should be directly made in favor of Abode Properties in the form of Cheque or Demand Draft. The bank account details will be provided once the booking is done.

The other charges after the project completion include (per flat) are ONLY,

  • Covered Car Parking – 1.50 Lakhs (Fixed)
  • BESCOM & BWSSB Deposit – 0.75 Lakhs (Fixed)
  • One Time Amenities Fee – 0.65 Lakhs (Fixed)

You will also have the option of “Flexi-Pay” that is, pay a higher initial amount and lesser EMI’s or fewer amount per EMI.
